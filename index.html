Nowadays, this role is known as an Solutions Architect; I research, plan and design digital transformations. Is that a buzzphrase? Sure it is, but it's exactly what I do.

Eckler's Automotive

eCommerce Architect/Lead Developer

2018 - 2021
I was hired by Ecklers to help migrate 11 Magento 1 instances with a combined 800k skus (and as many customers), to a single Magento 2 multistore instance, with several stores merging into single brands. There were lots of trials and tribulations with such a high sku count and the automotive industry is a tough one in general for eCommerce because of fitment (the data surrounding which vehicles parts fit). As the solutions architect, I helped refactor Magento modules and determine when and where to develop custom features vs leverage out of the box resources. I also implemented numerous automation scripts to help with the site
